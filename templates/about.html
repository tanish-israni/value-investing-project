About ValueInvest

ValueInvest is a smart, data-driven stock analysis platform built to empower investors in the Indian stock market to identify fundamentally undervalued companies.

Our core philosophy revolves around the principles of value investing. We meticulously analyze crucial financial ratios such as P/E, P/B, EV, EBITDA, EV/EBITDA to generate a percentile-based RV Score, highlighting companies with strong fundamental characteristics.

Engineered with precision using Python, Flask, and robust financial APIs, our platform enables users to:

  • Analyze companies within the Nifty 500 index.
  • Leverage a composite valuation score derived from multiple key metrics.
  • Download comprehensive investment reports in Excel format instantly.

Whether you are a student, a retail investor, or an aspiring quantitative analyst, ValueInvest offers a user-friendly yet powerful approach to discover high-quality stock ideas grounded in solid data.

Built with passion. Driven by logic. Designed for investors.
